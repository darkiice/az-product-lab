How taxes work

Every time you earn money, a little bit goes to taxes. Here's what's happening with your Daddy Bucks.

🤔 What are taxes for?
Taxes are a tiny share of what people earn that goes into a big pot. Families and the government use that pot to pay for things everyone shares — like:
🏠 Your house
💡 Electricity
💧 Water for your bath
🧼 Soap, food, clothes
🏫 School & books
🚒 Firefighters, parks, roads
So when you pay $1 in taxes, you're chipping in for the lights and the water and the library — really cool, right?
📦 Why does Cappie hold $2?
Cappie keeps $2 of every $20 in a safe spot — that's called withholding. The real tax is only $1. So when you file with TurboTax for Kids, the extra $1 comes right back to your balance as a refund! 🎉
