🎯 Training Principles

  • 80/20 rule. Most miles should feel easy. Save the hard efforts for tempo days.
  • Respect cutback weeks. Weeks 4 and 8 drop volume on purpose — let your body absorb the training.
  • Trust the taper. Weeks 11 and 12 will feel too easy. That's the point — you're storing it up.
  • Skip, don't stack. Miss a run? Move on. Never cram two hard days back-to-back to catch up.
